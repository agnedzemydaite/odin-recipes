Crustless Cranberry Pie

Description

This cranberry pie makes a light, delicious holiday dessert that's super simple to make with fresh or frozen cranberries. Serve warm with whipped cream or ice cream.

Ingredients

  • Flour: This cranberry pie starts with a cup of all-purpose flour.
  • Sugar: A cup of white sugar adds the perfect amount of sweetness.
  • Salt: You might not think a pinch of salt would make much of a difference, but you'll miss it if you don't use it.
  • Cranberries: Buy whole berries that are plump, firm, and unblemished. You can substitute frozen cranberries for fresh, but it will change the consistency of the batter, making it thicker.
  • Nuts: This recipe calls for walnuts, but you can pecans if you'd rather.
  • Eggs: Whole eggs add moisture and bind the batter together.
  • Almond extract: Almond extract lends a subtly complex and nutty flavor. You can substitute vanilla extract if you'd like.

Steps

  1. Place the apples in a baking dish.
  2. Sprinkle the apples with white sugar, flour, and cinnamon.
  3. Drizzle water over the apples.
  4. Make the oat topping, then sprinkle it over the apples.
  5. Bake until the topping is golden brown and the apple mixture is bubbly.
